🔄 How Recurring Billing Works
1
Restaurant signs up during onboarding
They choose a plan and enter their card details. PayFast tokenises the card — you never see the card number.
2
PayFast charges automatically every month
On the same date each month, PayFast debits the restaurant's card and deposits the subscription fee into your account.
3
Failed payments trigger automatic retry
PayFast retries failed payments and notifies you via webhook. The app shows a "payment overdue" warning to the restaurant owner.
4
You get paid, restaurants stay live
Settlement lands in your Coetzee Data AI Consultants bank account within 2–3 business days. Access is automatically suspended after 7 days of non-payment.
PayFast Recurring Billing API Endpoint
POST https://www.payfast.co.za/eng/process (sandbox: https://sandbox.payfast.co.za/eng/process)
Required fields: m_payment_id, merchant_id, merchant_key, amount, item_name, subscription_type=1, billing_date, recurring_amount, frequency=3 (monthly), cycles=0 (indefinite)
